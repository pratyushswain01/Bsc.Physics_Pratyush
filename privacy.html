Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy was last updated on July 20, 2025.

Our Commitment

The BSc. Physics Database ("the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This website is a static, read-only resource and is designed to function without collecting any personally identifiable information (PII) from its visitors. Your use of this service is anonymous.

Information We Collect (and Don't Collect)

We do not collect any personal data. There are no sign-up forms, no user accounts, no comment sections, and no analytics trackers that would gather your personal information such as name, email address, or IP address.

The only piece of data this website uses is your preference for the light or dark theme. This setting is saved in your web browser's localStorage. This information is stored exclusively on your device, is not transmitted to any server, and cannot be accessed by us.

Third-Party Services

This website uses a minimal number of third-party services to function, which may have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts: To provide the 'Inter' typeface for a clean design. You can view Google's Privacy Policy here.
  • Feather Icons: To display the icons used throughout the site. This is a library that runs locally in your browser.

We have not integrated any third-party analytics services like Google Analytics or advertising platforms.

Contact Us
