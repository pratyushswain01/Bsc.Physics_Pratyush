Course & Assessment Structure

The emphasis of this course is on applications in solving problems of interest to physicists. Students are examined on the basis of seen and unseen problems.

Course Outcomes

  • Conceptual understanding of Fourier series and its application.
  • Understanding the various special functions and their properties.
  • Understanding various polynomials and special integrations.
  • Learn the applications of partial differential equations.
  • Apply the acquired knowledge to solve problems.
