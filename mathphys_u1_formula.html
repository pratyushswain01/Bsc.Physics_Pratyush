Fourier Series & Parseval's Identity

This page presents a collection of essential Fourier series formulas. Each formula is displayed in its own card for clarity and easy reference.

General Fourier Series

1

Fourier Series in (0, 2π)

$$ f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} [a_n \cos(nx) + b_n \sin(nx)] $$

Coefficients:

$$ a_0 = \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} f(x) \,dx $$ $$ a_n = \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} f(x) \cos(nx) \,dx $$ $$ b_n = \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} f(x) \sin(nx) \,dx $$
2

Fourier Series in (-π, π)

$$ f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} [a_n \cos(nx) + b_n \sin(nx)] $$

Coefficients:

$$ a_0 = \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x) \,dx $$ $$ a_n = \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x) \cos(nx) \,dx $$ $$ b_n = \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x) \sin(nx) \,dx $$
3

Fourier Series in (0, 2L)

$$ f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left[ a_n \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) + b_n \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \right] $$

Coefficients:

$$ a_0 = \frac{1}{L} \int_{0}^{2L} f(x) \,dx $$ $$ a_n = \frac{1}{L} \int_{0}^{2L} f(x) \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \,dx $$ $$ b_n = \frac{1}{L} \int_{0}^{2L} f(x) \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \,dx $$
4

Fourier Series in (-L, L)

$$ f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left[ a_n \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) + b_n \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \right] $$

Coefficients:

$$ a_0 = \frac{1}{L} \int_{-L}^{L} f(x) \,dx $$ $$ a_n = \frac{1}{L} \int_{-L}^{L} f(x) \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \,dx $$ $$ b_n = \frac{1}{L} \int_{-L}^{L} f(x) \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \,dx $$

Series for Even and Odd Functions

5

Even Function in (-π, π) — Cosine Series

If $f(x)$ is even, all $b_n=0$.

$$ f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n \cos(nx) $$
$$ a_0 = \frac{2}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(x) \,dx $$ $$ a_n = \frac{2}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(x) \cos(nx) \,dx $$
6

Odd Function in (-π, π) — Sine Series

If $f(x)$ is odd, all $a_n=0$.

$$ f(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} b_n \sin(nx) $$
$$ b_n = \frac{2}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(x) \sin(nx) \,dx $$
7

Even Function in (-L, L) — Cosine Series

If $f(x)$ is even, all $b_n=0$.

$$ f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) $$
$$ a_0 = \frac{2}{L} \int_{0}^{L} f(x) \,dx $$ $$ a_n = \frac{2}{L} \int_{0}^{L} f(x) \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \,dx $$
8

Odd Function in (-L, L) — Sine Series

If $f(x)$ is odd, all $a_n=0$.

$$ f(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} b_n \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) $$
$$ b_n = \frac{2}{L} \int_{0}^{L} f(x) \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \,dx $$

Half-Range Series

9

Half-Range Cosine Series in (0, π)

$$ f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n \cos(nx) $$
$$ a_0 = \frac{2}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(x) \,dx \quad , \quad a_n = \frac{2}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(x) \cos(nx) \,dx $$
10

Half-Range Sine Series in (0, π)

$$ f(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} b_n \sin(nx) $$
$$ b_n = \frac{2}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(x) \sin(nx) \,dx $$
11

Half-Range Cosine Series in (0, L)

$$ f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) $$
$$ a_0 = \frac{2}{L} \int_{0}^{L} f(x) \,dx \quad , \quad a_n = \frac{2}{L} \int_{0}^{L} f(x) \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \,dx $$
12

Half-Range Sine Series in (0, L)

$$ f(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} b_n \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) $$
$$ b_n = \frac{2}{L} \int_{0}^{L} f(x) \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \,dx $$

Parseval's Identity

13

Identity in (0, 2π)

$$ \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{0}^{2\pi} [f(x)]^2 \,dx = \frac{a_0^2}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (a_n^2 + b_n^2) $$
14

Identity in (-π, π)

$$ \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi} [f(x)]^2 \,dx = \frac{a_0^2}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (a_n^2 + b_n^2) $$
15

Identity in (0, 2L)

$$ \frac{1}{L} \int_{0}^{2L} [f(x)]^2 \,dx = \frac{a_0^2}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (a_n^2 + b_n^2) $$
16

Identity in (-L, L)

$$ \frac{1}{L}\int_{-L}^{L} [f(x)]^2 \,dx = \frac{a_0^2}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (a_n^2 + b_n^2) $$
17

Identity for Half-Range Cosine Series in (0, π)

$$ \frac{2}{\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi} [f(x)]^2 \,dx = \frac{a_0^2}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n^2 $$
18

Identity for Half-Range Sine Series in (0, π)

$$ \frac{2}{\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi} [f(x)]^2 \,dx = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} b_n^2 $$
19

Identity for Half-Range Cosine Series in (0, L)

$$ \frac{2}{L}\int_{0}^{L} [f(x)]^2 \,dx = \frac{a_0^2}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n^2 $$
20

Identity for Half-Range Sine Series in (0, L)

$$ \frac{2}{L}\int_{0}^{L} [f(x)]^2 \,dx = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} b_n^2 $$
