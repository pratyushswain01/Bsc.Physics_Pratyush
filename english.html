AEC-2 : English Communication

Back to semester 2

Unit 1: Introduction to Communication

Key Topics

  • Communication, its importance and factors (sender, receiver, channel, etc.).
  • Models of communication, the information gap principle.
  • Types of communication: horizontal, vertical, interpersonal, lateral.
  • Verbal and non-verbal communication, body language, and bias-free language.

Unit 2: Listening & Speaking Skills

Key Topics

  • Types of listening: active, passive, empathic, interactive.
  • Speaking effectively: fluency, accuracy, intelligibility, and clarity.
  • Styles of speaking for various situations.
  • English pronunciation essentials: IPA, stress, rhythm, and intonation.

Unit 3: Reading & Writing Comprehension

Key Topics

  • Reading methods and techniques: skimming, scanning, global/local reading.
  • Understanding literal, metaphorical, and suggested meanings; identifying authorial tone.
  • The complete writing process: from brainstorming to post-writing.
  • Writing various forms: paragraphs, literary, academic, and media writing.

Unit 4: Grammar & Vocabulary Building

Key Topics

  • Applying grammar for meaning and effective communication.
  • Mastering verbs, modals, tense, aspect, voice, and modality.
  • Advanced sentence structures: complex noun phrases, clauses, and sentence types.
  • Building a usage-oriented and neutral vocabulary.
